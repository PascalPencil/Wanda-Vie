Bio

Born into a very musical family, my father was a jazz pianist and my 3 siblings play keys and sing.
When I was 13 I started singing in a band, and many more bands followed.
I sing all kinds of music, but if I get to choose I'd always go back to my favourite kind; blues and rock.
I've done some studio work and background vocals for some of "the greats" in the music business.
I Love going to jams and teaching.

Influenced by:
Janis Joplin, Ella Fitzgerald, Stevie Ray Vaughan, Rush , Gary Moore, Frank Zappa, Carly Simon, and many others.
